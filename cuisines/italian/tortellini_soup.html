Tortellini Soup

Cuisine:Italian

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Calories: 327 per serving

Total Time:50 minutes

Prep Time:10 minutes


327 Calories:
13g Fat:
38g Carbs:
16g Protein:

Tortellini soup is a simple yet delicious Italian soup. It combines the joys of cheese tortellini, fresh Italian sausage, and spinach for a delectable meal. It hits best on a cold night, or a lazy night in. Enjoy!

#StreetFood #Entree

Instructions:

  1. Pour one can of broth into a saucepan and add tortellini. Boil and cook until tender (about 5 minutes).
  2. Heat oil in large pot over medium heat. Cook and mix sausage, onion, garlic, and Italian seasoning until sausage is brown and crumbly (5-10 minutes).
  3. Add tortellini mixture, rest of broth, tomato puree, and spinach to sausage.
  4. Cover pot and simmer until flavors blend (about 30 minutes).

Chicken Madras

Cuisine:Indian

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Total Time: 20 minutes

Prep Time: 55 minutes

Chicken Madras is a British Indian style dish that's equal parts creamy, spicy, and savory. The mix of coconut milk and a variety of Indian spices makes for a flavorful dish. The spices can vary, but we bring you only the best! Enjoy!

Instructions:

  1. Combine the red chiles, cinnamon, cloves, coriander, fennel, peppercorns, and cumin in a skillet. Toast and toss for 3/4 minutes, then grind to powder.
  2. Pour tomatoes with juices into bowl, then rip tomatoes into 3/4 pieces each.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in large pot over medium heat. Add onion and ½ tsp salt and cook 8-10 minutes.
  4. Add green chiles, garlic, and ginger and cook for about another minute (until fragrant).
  5. Add chicken with rest of salt and stir. Then stir in spice mix, tomatoes + tomato juice, and coconut milk. Boil on high heat, then simmer on medium-low until chicken is cooked and sauce thickens.
  6. Serve atop rice. Top with cilantro if desired.

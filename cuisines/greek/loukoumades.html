Loukoumades

Cuisine: Greek (Dessert)

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Calories: 167 per serving

Total Time:1 hour 45 minutes

Prep Time: 1 hour


167 Calories:
7g Fat:
24g Carbs:
3g Protein:

Loukoumades are spicy sweet honey balls that originated in Greece, but are enjoyed all across the Mediterranean. They come topped with walnuts and honey and drizzled in honey sauce. These are the perfect dessert for any Greek meal! Enjoy!

Instructions:

  1. Stir together the water, yeast, and teaspoon of sugar, and let stand for 5 minutes (until foamy).
  2. Whisk the yeast mixture, milk, olive oil, salt, and rest of the sugar in a large bowl. Add flour and stir until smooth.
  3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and let it stand in a warm place until it doubles in size (about an hour).
  4. Pour 2 inches of oil into a Dutch pot and heat to 330°F. While it heats, line a baking sheet with paper towels.
  5. Use a sprayed cookie scoop to scoop balls from the dough. Cook in batches of 4-6, until golden brown. Place on lined baking sheet.
  6. While the loukoumades cook, melt the butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add the honey and cinnamon and stir.
  7. Place half the loukoumades in a large bowl, and toss with the honey mixture; repeat with the other half. Sprinkle with walnuts and serve immediately.

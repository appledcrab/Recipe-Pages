Big Plate Chicken

Cuisine: Chinese

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Calories: 560 per serving

Total Time: 50 minutes

Prep Time: 20 minutes


564 Calories:
18g Fat:
64g Carbs:
36g Protein:

Big plate chicken hails from the Chinese province of Xinjiang. It includes a variety of vegetables cooked alongside bone-in chicken, spices, and noodles. We've removed the bones for you, but left the savory punch. Enjoy!

#Spicy #Dinner

Got Everything You Need?

Instructions:

  1. Cut chicken into bite sized pieces. Transfer to bowl with the next 4 ingredients on the list for 20 minutes while you prep the other ingredients.
  2. Heat oil in wok over low heat. Add the star anise, cinnamon stick, bay leaves, ginger, garlic, Sichuan peppercorns, chilies, and sugar. Cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken and increase the heat. Stir-fry until the chicken is seared.
  4. Add the potatoes and carrots and stir-fry for another minute. Add the dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, shaoxing wine, and 3 cups water. Bring to boil, then reduce heat to medium.
  5. Cover wok and cook for 15 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
  6. While chicken cooks, prepare and drain noodles.
  7. While maintaining at least a cup of liquid in the wok, stir in bell peppers and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Season with salt to taste. Transfer noodles to plate and cover with chicken + vegetables. Sprinkle with scallions and serve immediately.
